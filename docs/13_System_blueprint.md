# Platform Overview

JVP Connect is a full-stack digital platform developed for Jumuiya ya Vijana wa Pwani (JVP).

The platform provides integrated digital services including membership management, leadership administration, event management, financial management, communication, partnerships, and organizational reporting.

The system is designed to serve as the official digital headquarters of JVP.
